Welcome to Rock-Paper-Scissors.


This the the classic game that we've all been playing since childhood.


- You will be playing against the computer.

- To play, just press the start button and take your pick from the options at the bottom.

- Yellow colour represents "Rock / Stone"

- Rose colour represents "Paper"

- Violet colour represents "Scissors"


The rules are as follows:-

  • First one to take 5 victories wins the game.
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper Beats Rock
